The Table Tennis Club of IISER Kolkata is run, managed and programmed entirely by IISER Kolkata students. With this nascent initiative, we as a fraternity want to provide this platform to one and all. Our goal is to make people know about our game - Table Tennis - and to unveil the students' hidden interests towards it, sharpen their skills, and promote whiff-whaff in the institution and beyond. This website includes useful information such as current events, tournament schedules, our awards and victories, and official rules. It has been designed and built by Satvik Saha and Ananyapam De.

Our logo

The official logo of the Table Tennis Club has been designed by Shruti Mandal (19MS). The bicolour logo keeps in focus the red and black sides of a TT racket, as well as the fact that the game is played between two teams. The IISER Kolkata logo also features, in the form of the double helix. Here, the letter 'i' represents our institution, the dot represents the ball in action, and the movement represents the victory of the game. The two 'T's form a table tennis table.
