The Doubles Tournament aims at testing coordination and compatibility between two players. The tournament is further subdivided into Boys' Doubles, Girls' Doubles and Mixed Doubles. Participation by our faculty is encouraged.
Each match consists of 5 sets, where the first pair to win 3 sets wins the match. The tournament is conducted over three weeks in the second half of the Spring semester.
