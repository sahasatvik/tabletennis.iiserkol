The Inter IISER Sports Meets (IISMs) are conducted between the 7 IISERS, NISER Bhubaneshwar, CEBS Mumbai, and IISC Bangalore.
The teams sent for the IISM generally consist of 4 or 5 members. The top 2 players in the ratings list are selected directly, and the remaining members are chosen either from the results of a tournament or by the coach.
