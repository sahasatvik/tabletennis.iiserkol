The Freshers Tournament is aimed at getting first year students acquainted with the club.
The first part of the tournament is played in singles, with 5 sets per match barring the final which has 7 sets. The second part is played with all first year students as a team against the rest of IISER Kolkata. This tournament is conducted over two weeks in the beginning of the Autumn semester.
