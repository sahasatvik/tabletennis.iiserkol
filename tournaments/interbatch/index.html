The Inter Batch Tournament has players from different batches competing with each other in teams. Each team contains between 2 and 4 players from the same batch (exceptions are made for PhD and IPhD students).
The teams are initially divided into pools, and each pool plays a league. The top two teams after the league matches enter the semi-finals, after which the winners play the finals and the losers play for third place. Matches are conducted according to the Corbillon Cup system, with 4 singles and 1 doubles match. This tournament is conducted over three weeks in the Autumn semester.
The 'Best Player of the Tournament' award is given to the player contributing the most in winning causes. The winning team of the tournament will eventually play against the rest of IISER Kolkata.
